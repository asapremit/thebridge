Frequently Asked Questions

How does same Path match me with an advisor?

Our intelligent matching system analyzes your specific needs, goals, and background to connect you with verified local advisors who have successfully navigated the exact challenges you are currently facing.

Yes. Every advisor on our platform goes through a rigorous vetting process, including credential verification, background checks, and community reviews to ensure you receive the highest quality guidance.

How do 1-on-1 bookings work?

Once matched, you can view your advisor's availability and book a secure video consultation directly through the platform. You'll also get access to a private message center and shared document vault.

Need more help?

If you can't find the answers you're looking for, our support team is available 24/7 to assist you. Reach out to us directly.
