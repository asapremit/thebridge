Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2026

At same Path, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform to connect with local advisors.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, complete our matching quiz, or communicate with advisors. This includes your name, email address, background context, and any documents you upload to your secure vault.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is primarily used to provide you with accurate advisor matches. We also use your information to facilitate secure communication between you and your advisor, process payments, and improve our matchmaking algorithms.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal data. We only share relevant details with the specific advisor you choose to book, and only to the extent necessary for them to assist you. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard security protocols.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can manage your information directly through your account settings or by contacting our support team at privacy@samepath.com.
